  • Carry Vietnamese Dong – many places are cash-only
  • Use Grab (app) for reliable transportation
  • Street food is safe if the stall is busy (locals know best!)
  • Respect temple dress codes – cover shoulders/knees
  • Keep mosquito repellent handy, especially in Phu Quoc
  • December is dry and cool — bring light layers
  • Always agree on a price before buying or riding
  • Basic Vietnamese phrases go a long way (like “cảm ơn” = thank you)
