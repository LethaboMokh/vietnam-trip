Useful Travel Tips

  • Weather: December is cool and dry in Hanoi (~15–22°C) and warm in Phu Quoc (~25–30°C). Layer wisely.
  • Currency: Vietnamese Dong (VND). Carry cash for markets, but most big stores accept cards.
  • Connectivity: Get a local SIM card at the airport for cheap data. Viettel is reliable.
  • Shopping: Great buys include silk, coffee, lanterns, and local snacks. Bargain gently at markets!
  • Etiquette: Dress modestly at temples, remove shoes when entering homes or certain places.
  • Sunscreen & Mosquito Repellent: Must-haves, especially in Phu Quoc.
