Vietnam Travel Tips

☁️ Weather in December

  • Hanoi: Cool and dry, 15–22°C. Bring a light jacket or windbreaker for mornings and evenings.
  • Phu Quoc: Warm, 25–30°C. Beach-perfect weather, but occasional brief showers possible.

🎒 What to Pack

  • Layers – especially for Hanoi’s cooler evenings
  • Swimsuits and beachwear for Phu Quoc
  • Comfortable walking shoes (markets, steps at Ninh Binh)
  • Reusable water bottle and hand sanitiser
  • Power bank and international plug adapter (types A, C, D)

🛍️ Where to Shop

  • Hanoi: Dong Xuan Market, Hanoi Night Market (great for local finds)
  • Phu Quoc: Night Market (souvenirs, snacks, local crafts)
  • Mini-marts (Circle K, VinMart) for basics

💡 Travel Tips

  • Cash is king – keep small bills for street food and taxis
  • Download Grab app for easy transport in cities
  • Google Translate & Google Maps will be lifesavers
  • Respect local culture – modest dress at temples, remove shoes
  • Don't drink tap water – always use bottled water

📲 Handy Apps

  • Grab: Local Uber for rides + food delivery
  • Google Translate: Camera feature helps read signs
  • Maps.me: Offline maps if WiFi is spotty
