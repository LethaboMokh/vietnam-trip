📅 12 Dec – Departure Day

Wear your airport outfit. Long travel day, settle in for arrival in Hanoi (evening).

📅 13 Dec – Arrival & Dinner

Arrive in Hanoi. Check in, shower, unwind. Evening dinner fit – casual and comfy for a relaxed intro to the city.

📅 14 Dec – Coffee Shops + Food Tour

Start the day with a relaxed fit. Visit Old Quarter, coffee shops, then poolside relaxation. Perfect night for a food tour & night market.

📅 15 Dec – Ha Long Bay

Ha Long Bay luxury day cruise. Outfit: comfy, layered. Expect photos, breeze, and a scenic day on the water.

📅 16 Dec – Chill Day

Take it slow. Sleep in, enjoy hotel facilities, maybe visit a rooftop bar or observation deck. Poolside relaxation.

📅 17 Dec – Ninh Binh

Day trip to Ninh Binh: boat ride, steps to viewpoint. Activewear & trainers essential. Early morning departure.

📅 18 Dec – Travel to Phu Quoc

Fly from Hanoi to Phu Quoc. Settle in and relax at hotel’s private beach. Evening: Phu Quoc Night Market.

📅 19 Dec – Explore Phu Quoc

Beach strolls, discover cafes and bars. Day is yours to explore. Outfit: relaxed, resort style.

📅 20 Dec – Island Hopping

Hon Thom cable car & 4-island hopping. Snorkelling gear, beachwear, dry bag and water shoes recommended.

📅 21 Dec – Resort Day

Spa, pool, journaling, enjoy your hotel. Slow it all down before the flight day.

📅 22 Dec – Travel to Hanoi

Fly to Hanoi or Johannesburg (depends on flight). Outfit: flight layers and comfort-focused.

📅 23 Dec – Layover Day

Long layover – wear something easy, layer up, keep charger and entertainment handy.
