Outfit Planner: Vietnam Edition

12 Dec – Airport Look

Comfy layered outfit for the long haul flight – leggings, sneakers, hoodie, and oversized scarf.

13 Dec – Dinner Fit

Relaxed but cute: wide-leg trousers, fitted top, and statement earrings for your first night in Hanoi.

14 Dec – Chill + Food Tour

Linen set or flowy dress for exploring cafés, the Old Quarter, and pool lounging before the night market.

15 Dec – Ha Long Bay

Breathable jumpsuit or activewear + windbreaker. Easy layers for boat breeze & photos on deck.

16 Dec – Rooftop & Chill

Kaftan or cotton lounge set + sandals. Perfect for strolling to the observation deck or chilling at the hotel.

17 Dec – Ninh Binh

Light activewear, trainers, sun hat. Ideal for boat rides & climbing steps to the viewpoint.

18 Dec – Hotel Beach

Swimsuit + coverup or beach maxi. Relax on your private beach, then dress up lightly for the night market.

19 Dec – Beach & Bars

Resort wear or shorts + cute top. Easy-going outfit for drinks and wandering along the shoreline.

20 Dec – Island Hopping

Swimsuit, rash guard, water shoes. Snorkel ready with a dry bag and sun hat.

21 Dec – Resort Day

Spa robe, loungewear, or cute PJs. Today’s for room service, spa, and soaking in your hotel stay.

22–23 Dec – Long Flight Home

Comfort-first layers for airport naps: oversized hoodie, joggers, socks, and crossbody bag.
